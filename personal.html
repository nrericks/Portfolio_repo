Remembrance Mural
Akira Toriyama

This is a mural dedicated to the memory of Akira Toriyama, the creator many manga series, including the Dragon Ball series. I created this mural as a personal project to honor his legacy and the impact he had on my life and the lives of many others. The mural features some of his most iconic characters and it serves as a tribute to his incredible talent and creativity. I hope that this mural can bring joy and inspiration to those who see it, just as Akira Toriyama's work has brought joy and inspiration to me.

This was not made for commercial use. None of the content was generated by AI, and all elements were obtained through legal means.
