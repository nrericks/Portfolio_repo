Movie Poster
Barbie (2023)

This is a mock movie poster I made for the movie Barbie (2023) as a personal project. I used Photoshop to create this poster, and I had a lot of fun making it. I wanted to create a poster that was colorful and eye-catching, and I think I succeeded in that. I also wanted to capture the humor of the movie, so I chose to use elements from one of the more comedic scenes. Overall, I'm really happy with how this poster turned out, and I'm proud of the work I put into it.

This was made for an academic assignment and not for any commercial use. This poster is not affiliated with the movie or its creators in any way. It is purely a fan-made project for educational purposes. None of the content was generated by AI, and all elements were obtained through legal means.

Date Made: March 2024
